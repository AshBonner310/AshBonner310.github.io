From Mathematics to Soil Science

After spending several years in graduate school studying Nonlinear Dynamics and other subjects, in 2022 I was given the opportunity to join the Todd-Brown lab and apply my research to the science of modeling soil carbon with a focus on how soil is modeled in earth systems models.

This website is still in a development stage. If you're interested in learning more about my work, please email me at BonnerA@ufl.ed
